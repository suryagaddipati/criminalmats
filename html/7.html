Comfort Flow Grit

ComfortFlow Grit mats are constructed of High Density Closed Cell Nitrile Rubber Cushion that has 15% recycled content which is far superior in grease and oil resistance than other “grease proof” mats. Low profile, 3/8 inch thick, and slip-resistant surface with added aggressive silicon carbide anti-slip top surface reduces slip and fall accidents. Non-skid backing keeps mat in place. ComfortFlow Grit mats are Anti-microbial Treated - The anti-microbial treatment guards against degradation from micro-organisms and minimizes mats being a source of odors. Providing excellent anti-fatigue properties for added employee comfort to improve employee morale and productivity, these mats meet all standards set in the Americans with Disabilities Act. ComfortFlow Grit mats are fully launderable and perform great in wet or dry environments.

Waterhog ECO Premier Tile

Waterhog Premier Tile has a 50% heavier face yarn than Waterhog Classic Tile, which makes it ideal for use in the most demanding applications. It is available in three patterns: Waterhog Premier Diagonal, Diamond and Geometric Tile. These tiles are suitable for high-traffic areas such as hospitals, grocery stores, airports, retail stores, schools, hotels and any other commercial buildings.

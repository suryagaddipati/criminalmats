Safety Scrape

Excellent traction and scraper mat all in one! The Safety Scrape is constructed of 100% Nitrile rubber construction to offer oil and chemical resistance. The circular grip-surface cleats effectively scrape from shoes and improve traction. Safety Scrape is perfect for exterior entrances as a scraper and as a slip-resistant mat in wet area applications. For added safety, the mats are available with OSHA color-coded borders to identify sensitive areas: Green for Saftey, Yellow for Caution, Orange for Warning and Red for Danger. Certified Slip Resistant by the National Floor Safety Institute.
